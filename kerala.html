Welcome to Kerala

God's Own Country

Kerala, known as 'God's Own Country', is located in the southern part of India. It is famous for its beautiful backwaters, lush landscapes, pristine beaches, and vibrant culture.

Some top attractions in Kerala include:

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Fort Kochi
  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Munnar Tea Gardens
  • Varkala Beach

Kerala offers a perfect blend of scenic beauty, adventure, and cultural richness.
